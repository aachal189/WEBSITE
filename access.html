ACCESS OPTIONS


Direct tasking

Earth observation from your site via an allocated satellite access window Gain unparalleled control over satellite operations with direct tasking capabilities. Using an allocated satellite access window, you can schedule and prioritize Earth observation tasks specific to your needs. This process enables real-time data acquisition and ensures that satellite resources are used efficiently to capture high-resolution imagery and geospatial data.


Premium indirect tasking

Securely planned high-priority tasking executed Premium Indirect Tasking refers to a highly specialized service designed to cater to high-priority needs with precision and security. This service involves securely planned and expertly executed tasking operations conducted by Maxar, a global leader in geospatial intelligence and satellite technology.


Imagery archive ordering

View imagery from the world's largest archive, delivered to you via FTP Imagery Archive Ordering provides access to the world's largest collection of high-resolution satellite imagery, enabling users to explore a wealth of historical and contemporary geospatial data. This service is ideal for research, planning, analysis, and monitoring applications across various industries.
